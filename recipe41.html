Greek salad (Greek: χωριάτικη σαλάτα choriatiki salata [xorˈjatici saˈlata] "rustic salad" or θερινή σαλάτα therini salata [θeriˈni saˈlata] "summer salad") is a salad in Greek cuisine. Greek salad is made with pieces of tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, onion, feta cheese, and olives (usually Kalamata olives), typically seasoned with salt and oregano, and dressed with olive oil. Common additions include green bell pepper slices or berries of capers (especially in the Dodecanese islands). Notably, the Greek salad is often seen as a farmer's breakfast or lunch, as its ingredients resemble those that a Greek farmer might have on hand Ingredients:
3 – 4 tomatoes,
1 onion,
feta cheese,
1 cucumber
olive oil,
salt,
olives,
oregano,
Step:
Cut the tomatoes, the onion and the cucumber in slices.
Mix them.
Add little salt* and some pieces of feta cheese, oregano and at the end pour some olive oil.
